# 🚨 SENTINEL — Multi-Business Risk Intelligence Platform

> *“Predict. Prepare. Protect.”*

SENTINEL is an AI-powered platform that analyzes global events and predicts their impact on businesses across industries like **Food, Energy, and Manufacturing**.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

In today’s interconnected world, events like:

* Wars 🌐
* Natural disasters 🌪️
* Supply chain disruptions 🚢
* Economic shifts 💰

create **ripple effects** across industries.

However, most businesses:

* React **after damage is done**
* Lack **real-time intelligence**
* Cannot **simulate future risks**

---

## 💡 Solution

SENTINEL transforms raw global data into **actionable business intelligence**.

It:

* Monitors multi-source global data
* Analyzes impact using AI
* Predicts business risks
* Provides decision-ready insights

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🔍 Multi-Source Data Ingestion

* News (global events)
* Weather data
* Supply chain information
* Shipment/logistics data
* Commodity prices (real-time APIs)

---

### ⚡ Intelligent Risk Analysis

* Event → Impact mapping
* Industry-specific risk scoring
* Pattern recognition using AI/LLMs

---

### 📊 Business Impact Prediction

* Predicts:

  * Price fluctuations
  * Supply shortages
  * Delivery delays
* Generates **early warnings**

---

### 🔮 What-If Simulation

* Simulate scenarios like:

  * Increase in fuel price
  * Workforce changes
  * Supply disruptions
* Estimate:

  * Cost impact
  * Operational risk
  * Business runway

---

### 🚨 Smart Alerts

Example:

> ⚠️ “Cyclone in coastal region may disrupt rice supply → expect price increase in 7–10 days.”
